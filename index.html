  1. Setup
    • Divide players into two teams
    • Arrange players, alternating teams, in a circle
    • Select Category
    • Choose player to start

  2. Game
    • Upon start, the device will display a word or phrase
    • The player holding the device must give clues, gestures, actions, to describe the word or phrase
    • DO NOT SAY THE PHRASE OR ANY PART OF THE PHRASE
    • Their team guesses phrase displayed based on the hints
    • Once the phrase is guessed, pass the device to the next player
    • Whichever team is not holding the device when the timer ends receives a point
    • Pass to next player and continue

  3. Winning
    • First team to collect the maximum points wins (default 7)

  4. Tips
    • You can press "NEXT" as much as you want. If you can't describe the word or phrase, just go to the next phrase
    • Hot potato rules, if you're holding the device when the timer goes off, the other team gets a point. Pass it fast!
